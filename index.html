INVESTMENTS

Investment Focus

Reset Capital (RC) is an investment firm with a unique focus on investment opportunities in BEE companies that are created as a result of the Africaneur IQ (AIQ) RESET Model of Economic Transformation.

RESET stands for Rapid Equitable Sustainable Economic Transformation and is a proprietary model used by AIQ to facilitate economic transformation in South Africa under the Broad Based Black Economic Empowerment Legislation

The Beneficiary Companies in the RESET Model undergo rapid expansion and growth, typically underpinned by three to five-year offtake contracts and concessionary funding with the sole objective of increasing market share and taking a significant position in its particular sector. In order to rapidly expand the capacity of the designated beneficiary’s start-up, AIQ’s model harnesses the introduction of an early stage equity investor (usually between 40%-49%) to accelerate the capacity expansion. This traditionally white shareholder/s has a natural 24-36-month exit horizon. At that point RESET Capital enables the exit of the capacity accelerator and aims to assist the beneficiary for a potential listing within a further 24-36-months.

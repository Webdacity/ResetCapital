Reset Capital (RC) is an investment firm with a unique focus on investment opportunities in BEE companies that are created as a result of the Africaneur IQ (AIQ) RESET Model of Economic Transformation.

RESET stands for Rapid Equitable Sustainable Economic Transformation and is a proprietary model used by AIQ to facilitate economic transformation in South Africa under the Broad Based Black Economic Empowerment Legislation.


The Beneficiary Companies in the RESET Model undergo rapid expansion and growth, typically underpinned by three to five year offtake contracts and concessionary funding with the sole objective of increasing market share and taking a significant position in its particular sector RC will provide a dedicated mechanism for the natural exit of the Capacity Accelerators out of RESET Beneficiary Companies and also for provide ad hoc financial support to Beneficiary companies as and when required RC will provide equity and debt solutions to RESET Beneficiary Companies Through this document, RC seeks to raise R1bn in debt finance (R200m per Financial Services top 5) to fund its debt and equity investments in RESET Beneficiary Companies in compliance with FS600.

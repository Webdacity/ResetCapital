ABOUT

Reset Capital (RC) is an investment firm with a unique focus on investment opportunities in BEE companies that are created as a result of the Africaneur IQ (AIQ) RESET Model of Economic Transformation.

RESET stands for Rapid Equitable Sustainable Economic Transformation and is a proprietary model used by AIQ to facilitate economic transformation in South Africa under the Broad Based Black Economic Empowerment Legislation.


The Beneficiary Companies in the RESET Model undergo rapid expansion and growth, typically underpinned by three to five year offtake contracts and concessionary funding with the sole objective of increasing market share and taking a significant position in its particular sector. RC will provide a dedicated mechanism for Beneficiary entrepreneur to cross the Transitional Transformation Capital gap initially and thereafter to allow for the natural exit of the Capacity Accelerators if required. RC also intends to provide ad hoc financial support to Beneficiary companies as and when required.

INVESTMENTS

Investment Focus

Reset Capital (RC) is an investment firm with a unique focus on investment opportunities in BEE companies that are created as a result of the Africaneur IQ (AIQ) RESET Model of Economic Transformation.

The Beneficiary Companies in the RESET Model undergo rapid expansion and growth, typically underpinned by three to five-year offtake contracts and concessionary funding with the sole objective of increasing market share and taking a significant position in its particular sector.

In order to rapidly expand the capacity of the designated beneficiary’s start-up, AIQ’s model harnesses the introduction of an early stage equity investor (usually between 40%-49%) to accelerate the capacity expansion.

This methodology creates a unique initial capital requirement that arises between the time that the beneficiary entrepreneur creates the new start-up and when free cash-flows are generated from the blue-chip off take agreements having leveraged the capacity acceleration. This type of investment is referred to as Transitional Transformation Capital (TTC) is represents the first round of funding RC is focussed on.

The second round of investment (usually within 12-24 months from the first TTC) follows when the traditionally white shareholder/s of the capacity accelerator selects a natural exit. At that point RESET Capital is able to accommodate the capacity accelerator and aims to assist the beneficiary for a potential listing within a further 24-36-months.
